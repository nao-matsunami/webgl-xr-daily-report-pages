Use clicks or audio peaks to momentarily widen the focus field, refine nearby splats, and intensify artifact heat or chromatic breakup. Instead of visualizing audio constantly, use it as timing for spatial-quality updates, connecting naturally to July 7 prediction and July 8 history.

制作に使えそうな技術メモProduction notes

impulse は attack を速く、decay を少し遅くします。Web Audio を使う場合は analyser の peak/RMS を 0..1 に正規化し、shader uniform に渡します。今回の最小サンプルでは外部 permission を避けるため、合成 beat とクリック impulse を使い、同じ設計で mic/audio input に差し替えられるようにしています。

Use a fast attack and slower decay for impulses. With Web Audio, normalize analyser peak/RMS to 0..1 and pass it as a shader uniform. Today's minimal sample avoids external permissions by using a synthetic beat and click impulses, but the same design can be swapped to microphone or audio input.
