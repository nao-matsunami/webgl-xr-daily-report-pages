注目事例と何が面白いかNotable examples and why they matter

Vis4GS が面白いのは、Gaussian splat を『きれいに見る対象』から『診断できる素材』へ変えている点です。View Coverage、blur score、needle-like artifact、clone/split/prune の履歴を見られると、表現側でも単なる LOD ではなく、どの部分を意図的に粗くするか、どこだけ観客に読ませるか、どの artifact を演出に転用するかを決めやすくなります。SuperSplat のような編集ツールと組み合わせると、スキャン空間に情報膜や焦点レンズを重ねるだけでなく、splat の弱点そのものをインタラクションの手がかりにできます。

Vis4GS is interesting because it turns Gaussian splats from something to view beautifully into material that can be diagnosed. When View Coverage, blur score, needle-like artifacts, and clone/split/prune history become visible, expression work can go beyond LOD and decide what should remain coarse, where viewers should be allowed to read, and which artifacts can become part of the effect. Combined with tools like SuperSplat, scanned spaces can use their own weak points as interaction cues rather than just placing information membranes or focus lenses on top.

表現・インタラクションのアイデアExpression and interaction ideas

短期では、前回の adaptive MSDF / grain field を『診断レンズ』に変換します。ポインタ周辺だけ coverage ring、blur/needle heat、splat halo を出し、焦点外では空間が通常の粒子景観に戻る構成です。中期では、HTMLTexture の注釈をこのレンズ内だけ表示し、artifact score が高い splat にだけ警告や詩的なラベルを出すと、スキャン空間の欠落を作品の情報設計にできます。長期では、XR で背景 splat layer、診断 layer、注釈 layer を分け、視線・手・音で診断モードを浮上させる方向が有望です。

In the short term, convert the previous adaptive MSDF / grain field into a diagnostic lens. Show coverage rings, blur/needle heat, and splat halos only around the pointer, while the unfocused area returns to a particle landscape. In the mid term, show HTMLTexture annotations only inside this lens and attach warnings or poetic labels to high-artifact splats, turning scan defects into information design. In the long term, separate background splat layers, diagnostic layers, and annotation layers in XR, letting gaze, hand movement, or audio bring diagnostic mode to the surface.

制作に使えそうな技術メモProduction notes

focus field は今回も 0..1 の scalar として扱い、splat の halo 強度、coverage ring の透明度、artifact heat の色、scanline 密度へ配ると拡張しやすいです。実データに進む場合は、各 splat に opacity、scale anisotropy、screen-space footprint、view count 由来の coverage を持たせ、lens 内だけ attribute texture から診断値を読む構成が軽いです。HTML-in-Canvas と組み合わせる場合は、paint/requestPaint の更新頻度を lens 内だけに絞る発想が有効で、XR Layers では診断表示を quad/cylinder/equirect など別レイヤーへ逃がすと背景の splat 品質と UI 可読性を分けられます。

Keep the focus field as a 0..1 scalar and distribute it to splat halo intensity, coverage-ring opacity, artifact-heat color, and scanline density. With real data, attach opacity, scale anisotropy, screen-space footprint, and view-count-derived coverage to each splat, then read diagnostic values from an attribute texture only inside the lens. With HTML-in-Canvas, limit paint/requestPaint updates to the lens region; in XR Layers, move diagnostic display into a separate quad, cylinder, or equirect layer so background splat quality and UI readability can be tuned separately.

俯瞰メモBird's-eye memo

最近の流れは『情報を GPU texture にする』、『注意が向いた場所だけ高品質化する』に続き、『品質や artifact の理由を見せる』へ進められます。HTML-in-Canvas は情報面、MSDF は可読性、GSplat/foveated culling は空間品質配分、Vis4GS は診断軸、WebXR Layers は表示分離を担えます。組み合わせ候補は、1. splat diagnostic lens + HTMLTexture 注釈、2. artifact score + 音反応、3. coverage heat + gaze/focus field、4. SuperSplat 編集履歴 + 時間方向の shader、5. XR Layers + 診断 mode の出し入れ。育てる価値が高いテーマは『欠陥を隠す最適化』ではなく『欠陥を読める演出へ変える最適化』です。

The recent path can move from making information into GPU textures, to raising quality only where attention arrives, and now to showing why quality or artifacts exist. HTML-in-Canvas provides information surfaces, MSDF provides readability, GSplat/foveated culling provides spatial quality allocation, Vis4GS provides diagnostic axes, and WebXR Layers provides display separation. Promising combinations are: splat diagnostic lens plus HTMLTexture annotations, artifact score plus audio reactivity, coverage heat plus gaze/focus fields, SuperSplat edit history plus temporal shaders, and XR Layers plus toggled diagnostic modes. The high-value theme is not optimization that hides defects, but optimization that turns defects into readable expression.
