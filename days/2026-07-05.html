The notable pattern today is that PlayCanvas v2.20 places HTML-in-Canvas texture upload, GSplat foveated culling/stereo XR, and MSDF anti-aliasing fixes in the same broader stream. This suggests readable text, heavy splat spaces, and gaze-near quality allocation can be treated as one quality-distribution problem rather than separate features. three.js r185 HTMLTexture and TSL storageTexture3D open the door to treating information surfaces not as flat 2D panels but as layered materials across time or depth.

表現・インタラクションのアイデアExpression and interaction ideas

短期では、前回の SDF Focus Splat Membrane を『可変 AA の文字膜』へ寄せるのが良いです。焦点近傍では文字の edge AA、粒子 cell size、scanline 密度を上げ、焦点外では情報を読めない質感に落とします。中期では、HTMLTexture 由来の注釈、MSDF 文字、GSplat の LOD/foveated culling を同じ focus field で結び、視線や手が近い場所だけ情報と空間の品質が同時に立ち上がる演出にできます。長期では、WebXR Layers で背景空間、情報膜、メディア面を分け、読みやすさと没入感をレイヤーごとに最適化する方向が育てられます。

In the short term, extend the previous SDF Focus Splat Membrane toward a variable-AA text membrane. Near focus, raise edge AA quality, particle cell density, and scanline density; outside focus, let the information fall back into an unreadable material texture. In the mid term, bind HTMLTexture annotations, MSDF text, and GSplat LOD/foveated culling through the same focus field so information and spatial quality rise together near gaze or hands. In the long term, WebXR Layers could separate background space, information membranes, and media planes, optimizing readability and immersion per layer.

制作に使えそうな技術メモProduction notes

実装では focus を 0..1 の scalar field として持ち、文字の smoothstep 幅、粒子グリッドの cell size、texture mip/LOD、sRGB/linear 変換後の発色、post-process 強度へ配ると扱いやすいです。MSDF/SDF 文字は UV magnification と field gradient のどちらを AA 基準にするかで見え方が変わるため、焦点外の blur と焦点内の edge を別パラメータに分けておくと調整しやすいです。HTML-in-Canvas を使う場合は、DOM snapshot の paint/requestPaint、texElementImage2D/copyElementImageToTexture の転送頻度、DOM hit testing と描画位置同期を、描画品質とは別に測る必要があります。

Implementation-wise, keep focus as a 0..1 scalar field and distribute it to text smoothstep width, particle-grid cell size, texture mip/LOD, post-sRGB/linear color response, and post-process intensity. For MSDF/SDF text, the look changes depending on whether AA is driven by UV magnification or field gradient, so keep out-of-focus blur and in-focus edge sharpness as separate parameters. With HTML-in-Canvas, measure DOM snapshot paint/requestPaint behavior, texElementImage2D/copyElementImageToTexture transfer frequency, and synchronization between DOM hit testing and drawn placement separately from visual quality.

俯瞰メモBird's-eye memo

最近の傾向は『情報を GPU 素材にする』から一段進んで、『限られた解像度と視線近傍の品質をどう配分するか』へ移っています。HTML-in-Canvas は素材化、Babylon.js の sRGB 修正は色の信頼性、PlayCanvas の GSplat/foveated/MSDF 修正は空間と文字の品質配分、WebXR Layers は表示面の分離に効きます。組み合わせ候補は、1. HTMLTexture + 可変 AA、2. MSDF text + focus field、3. GSplat foveated culling + readable membrane、4. WebXR Layers + 情報膜、5. 音反応 + 焦点半径。育てる価値が高いテーマは『読める品質を常時出さず、注意が向いた時だけ立ち上げる』ことです。

The recent trend is moving from turning information into GPU material toward deciding how limited resolution and near-gaze quality should be allocated. HTML-in-Canvas materializes information; Babylon.js sRGB fixes support color reliability; PlayCanvas GSplat/foveated/MSDF work supports quality allocation across space and text; WebXR Layers helps separate display surfaces. Promising combinations are: HTMLTexture plus variable AA, MSDF text plus focus fields, GSplat foveated culling plus readable membranes, WebXR Layers plus information membranes, and audio reactivity plus focus radius. The high-value theme is not rendering readable quality all the time, but raising it only when attention arrives.
